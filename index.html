Mama | Founder | CEO | Software Developer | Strategist | Author | Yoga Teacher | Scuba Diver | Marathon Runner | Influencer | Model

Kate Julia is from Detroit, MI. She has a Bachelor of Science in Accounting and Information Systems Management and a Master of Science in Entrepreneurship from the University of Washington, Foster School of Business. She worked at Deloitte as a consultant with Fortune 500 companies. She is a certified Yoga instructor and specializes in women with sexual trauma giving birth. She launched Hammerhead Lingerie, SPC recently, and is empowering women to build a seat at THE table. However, her most important job in this world; a single mother to her 3-year-old daughter diagnosed with Autism. She is working to bridge the gap between gender and visible and invisible disabilities in our world.
